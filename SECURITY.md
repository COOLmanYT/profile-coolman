# Security Policy

## Supported Versions
The following versions of our project are actively maintained and receive security updates:
- Latest version
- Previous version

## How to Report Vulnerabilities
To report a security vulnerability, please contact us directly at our obfuscated email: official.coolmanyt.com[@]gmail.com.

## Response Timelines
We aim to respond to all security reports within 5 business days. If you’ve not received a response within this timeframe, please follow up with us.

## Disclosure Policy
We consider responsible disclosure to be a priority. Please do not publicly disclose any vulnerabilities until we have had a chance to address them.

## What to Include in a Report
When reporting a vulnerability, please include:
- A detailed description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce the issue
- Impact assessment

## Safe Harbor
We appreciate your efforts to responsibly disclose vulnerabilities and will make every effort to protect your anonymity. We will not take legal action against you for your good faith efforts to report security vulnerabilities.
